A Setlist Made by the Crowd

🗳

Vote

Tablets hang from the ceiling around the venue. Pick the next dance style — Waltz, Polka, Mazurka, Jig, Branles and more.

📽

Watch

Votes are projected live on a screen behind the musicians. See the bars fill up in real time as the crowd decides.

💃

Dance

The winning dance is announced, the band launches into it, and the floor comes alive. Then you vote again.

How It Works

  1. 1

    Tablets descend

    During the current dance, tablets hanging from the ceiling light up with a menu of dance options.

  2. 2

    The audience votes

    Everyone taps their favourite dance. One vote per person, per round — fair and simple.

  3. 3

    Results go live on stage

    A big screen behind the band shows vote bars climbing in real time — the suspense is part of the show.

  4. 4

    The band plays the winner

    Voting closes, the winner is revealed, and the musicians kick off the chosen dance. Every set is unique.

About Balalacarte

Balalacarte is a folk band that believes the best dance nights are the ones shaped by the dancers themselves. Armed with accordion, guitar, bass, drums and a custom-built voting system, we turn every gig into a collaborative experience where the audience drives the setlist.

No two shows are ever the same — because no two crowds are ever the same.
